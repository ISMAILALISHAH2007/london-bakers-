Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?
We recommend ordering custom cakes at least 48 hours in advance. For wedding cakes or large events, please contact us 2-3 weeks ahead.
Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?
Yes! We have a selection of gluten-free and vegan pastries. Please specify your dietary requirements when ordering.
What is your delivery radius and fees?
We deliver within 20 miles of our atelier. Delivery is free for orders over $100, otherwise a $10 fee applies.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled up to 24 hours before delivery/pickup. For custom orders, modifications may incur additional charges.
