Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a custom cake?

We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance for standard custom cakes. For wedding cakes or large events, please contact us 2-3 weeks ahead to ensure availability.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within France and select European countries. For international inquiries, please contact us to discuss custom solutions.

Can I schedule a tasting appointment?

Yes! We offer complimentary tasting appointments for wedding cakes and large events. Please use the form above to schedule.

What are your dietary accommodation options?

We offer gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free options. Please specify any dietary requirements in your inquiry, and we'll work with you to create something delicious and safe.
